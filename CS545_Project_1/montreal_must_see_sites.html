Must See Sites

Mount Royal

A large hill and the highest point in Montreal. This area also includes a park (Parc du Mont-Royal) and at the summit, there is a breathtaking view of downtown Montreal.

Notre-Dame Basilica

Located in Old Montreal, this church showcases its Gothic architecture and its glass windows. The inside of the basilica is also complex and its construction is detailed with dim lights permeating its surroudings.

St. Joseph's Oratory

Canada's largest church, the steps to the building's entrance gives a great view of the neighborhood around it.
