Winter 2017

Half-Marathon

Race Details

Location

Please gather at the starting line which will be located outside the SDSU Library and in front of Hepner Hall!

Time

8:00am on Sunday, December 3, 2017

Please arrive and check in 30 minutes prior to the start of the race. Participants must clip on to the front of their shirt their bib which they should have received after showing proof of identification. Come prepared! Running shoes are recommended, casual shoes may be uncomfortable for running and exercise.

Registration

There are no payments are fees required to register for this event. This is a student-coordinated event that aims to provide an enjoyable experience for the Winter season. Please register via the register link in the menu above. Registration is necessary in order to identify the runners and provide medical conditions and issues. If you have a serious medical condition, are pregnant, or the state of your health is problematic, please be cautious during the marathon and take care not to physically overexert or stress your body. In these cases, it may be best not to attend. Late registrations will not be submitted. You can cancel your registration at any time.

Corral and Wave Start

At the start of the race, please position yourself based on the corral you belong to. It is recommended that fast and experienced runners situate themselves towards the front of the starting line. For those who are deciding to run casually or walk, please start towards the back. This will help prevent any accidental injuries that may occur.

Hydration Stations

There will be hydration stations throughout the marathon! Remember to pace yourself and stay hydrated prior to and throughout this event.

Race Course

The starting line will be located in front of Hepner Hall our on the field. You must have on you your bib prior to the start of the race. You can retrieve your bib at the SDSU Bookstore. The course spans about 12 miles and takes you around the college campus. Runners will loop around the campus 4 times as each loop is approximately 3 miles. Every time you pass the starting line, the number of laps will be recorded for your specific bib number. Most of what you will be running on is pavement and through driveways. Since this race uses these driveways, sections of streets will be closed off, including Hilltop Way, Avenue of Arts, Aztec Circle Drive (or Plysical Plant way), Canyon Crest Drive, parts of Remington Road and 55th street, Hardy Avenue, parts of Campanile Drive, Linda Paseo, and parts of College Avenue.

Race Rules

  1. You may not distribute, share, or give your bib in any sort of transaction to someone else.
  2. You may not sell or make any sort of profit from selling your bib to other individuals.
  3. You must be at least 13 years of age to attend. Seniors are allowed to register but take caution of your physical state during the run.
  4. You should be in good health in preparation for the race.
  5. You are required to present your ID to receive your bib.
  6. Pick up your bib at the SDSU Bookstore.
  7. Any sort of transportation vehicles or portable boards including bikes, skateboards, scooters, in-line skates, baby joggers, coaches, pets, and so on are not allowed.
  8. The time limit for the race is 4 hours, beginning after the last participant has crossed the starting line.
  9. A medical team will be on site in case first aid is necessary.
  10. Race times will be available online after the race, and an email will be sent to you linking you to your time.

At the end of the race, you will receive your very own gear bag! Refreshments and water will also be available.

Parking

Parking Structures 3, 4, 7, and 9 will be available for parking for this event. Please print your temporary parking permit and place on the front of your dashboard.
